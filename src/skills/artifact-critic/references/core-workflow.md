# Core Critique Workflow

Apply these steps for every artifact type.

## Step 1: Summarize

In 1-2 sentences:
- what is proposed,
- what problem it solves,
- what decision is being made now.

## Step 2: Surface assumptions

List explicit and hidden assumptions.
For each assumption include:
- validation method,
- failure impact if assumption is false.

## Step 3: Identify top risks

List 3-7 high-value risks across:
- technical,
- operational,
- security/privacy,
- delivery/time,
- cost/maintenance.

For each risk include:
- impact,
- likelihood,
- mitigation.

## Step 4: Present alternatives

Provide at least 2 realistic alternatives:
- one conservative (lower risk, faster adoption),
- one strategic (higher long-term value, more investment).

## Step 5: Evaluate trade-offs

Compare baseline and alternatives across:
- complexity,
- delivery speed,
- reliability,
- scalability,
- observability/operability,
- maintenance cost.

## Step 6: Recommend

Choose a preferred option and provide:
- why now,
- acceptance conditions,
- contingency or rollback plan.

## Step 7: Capture open questions

List unresolved questions that materially affect decision quality.

If information is missing, call it out explicitly instead of guessing.
