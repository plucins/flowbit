# Adapter: plan

## Focus questions

- Is sequencing realistic and dependency-aware?
- Are milestones tied to verifiable outcomes?
- Is scope controlled to prevent hidden expansion?
- Are risks and fallback actions mapped to specific steps?

## Common failure patterns

- Missing prerequisite tasks.
- Vague completion criteria ("done" without verification).
- Underestimated integration/testing effort.
- No recovery path when a key step fails.

## Extra acceptance conditions

- Dependencies and order are explicit.
- Each major step has measurable completion criteria.
- Includes explicit fallback path for critical steps.
