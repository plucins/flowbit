# Adapter: other

Fallback adapter for unknown artifact types.

## Focus questions

- What decision does this artifact influence?
- What assumptions must hold for it to be useful?
- What are the primary failure modes?
- What safer alternatives exist?

## Common failure patterns

- Ambiguous artifact purpose.
- Missing success criteria.
- No evidence supporting key claims.

## Extra acceptance conditions

- Artifact purpose is explicit.
- Success criteria are measurable.
- Recommendation confidence is proportional to available evidence.
