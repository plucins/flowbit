# Adapter: idea

## Focus questions

- What hypothesis creates value, and for whom?
- Is feasibility grounded in current constraints?
- What is the fastest testable version?
- What would disconfirm the idea quickly?

## Common failure patterns

- Value claim without measurable outcome.
- No path from concept to testable experiment.
- Solution-first framing without clear problem validation.
- Ignoring adoption and change-management risks.

## Extra acceptance conditions

- Defines one concrete success metric.
- Defines one falsification criterion.
- Defines smallest viable experiment.
