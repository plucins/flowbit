# Adapter: review-report

## Focus questions

- Is evidence sufficient to support conclusions?
- Are findings prioritized by severity and impact?
- Are recommendations actionable and scoped?
- Are assumptions, gaps, and residual risks explicit?

## Common failure patterns

- Opinion-heavy claims without evidence.
- Missing reproduction details for critical findings.
- Recommendations too generic to execute.
- No clear decision guidance (go/no-go/revise).

## Extra acceptance conditions

- Critical findings include evidence and concrete next action.
- Report distinguishes fact, assumption, and interpretation.
- Includes explicit residual risk statement.
